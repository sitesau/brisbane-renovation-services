Common questions

Do I need council approval? Many Brisbane renovations require development approval, especially structural changes, extensions, or heritage-affected properties. Always check with Brisbane City Council before starting.

How long does the process take? Project duration varies by scope. Simple bathroom updates may take 4 weeks, while comprehensive home additions can span 6-12 months including design, approvals, and construction.
